## Role Definitions

- **DRI**: Directly Responsible Individual - the single person accountable for ensuring the Priority delivers its intended outcomes. Driver of the team.
- **Contributor**: Individuals who are responsible for executing meaningful parts of the work. Part of the core team.
- **Consulted**: Individuals brought in for expertise, context or perspective to inform decisions or approach. Adjunct to the core team.
- **Informed**: Individuals or groups kept aware of progress, decisions or outcomes - for visibility only. Default channel: MBR.

## Engagement Tier Definitions

| Tier | What It Means | Time Commitment | Typical Engagement Model |
|------|---------------|-----------------|-------------------------|
| **Heavy (Embedded)** | Pseudo-embedded member of the core team. Deeply integrated into day-to-day work. | ~20% average, up to 50%+ during peak phases | Dedicated sync + embedded in team rituals (product review, standups) |
| **Medium (Inner Circle)** | Consulted regularly. Concentric circle around core team. | ~5-15% | Standard EPD rituals (product review, roadmap review) OR milestone-based check-ins |
| **Light (As-Needed)** | Consulted for specific decisions or phases. | ~1-5% | As-needed consultation + MBR updates |
