## üéØ Why We're Here

Our reporting doesn't give clients a clear, confident understanding of performance or direction. It's a reactive readout of marketing metrics rather than proactive multifamily strategy. This weakens client confidence, forces AMs to compensate with workarounds, and makes us vulnerable to competitors like StreetDigitalAI and PERQ Pulse who are delivering stronger storytelling.

Meanwhile, Fiona is no longer something clients (or us) point to as a differentiator. If we don't evolve now, we risk losing the confidence of our clients that we are tech leaders and experts in multifamily marketing.

## üé≤ Strategy

**How we'll get there:**

1. **Marketing ‚Üí Multifamily Story:** Show how marketing drives multifamily outcomes (leads, leases, occupancy, ROI) by integrating PMS/CRM data

2. **Reporting Redesign:** High-signal, simplified reporting directly in the native Fiona experience‚Äîmoving from dense data-dumps to clear storytelling

3. **Fiona AI:** Evolve from reactive read-outs to proactive storytelling & strategy via AI-powered executive summaries, insights, and recommendations

4. **Fiona Budgets 2.0:** Rebuild budget recommendations to be trusted, explainable, and actionable‚Äîshowing clients how to shift spend to hit their goals

## üî¨ Scope

**‚úÖ In Scope:**
- Changes to client-facing reporting (what clients see, how they interact with it)
- Changes to internal reporting workflows (how AMs prepare for and deliver client conversations)
- New data integrations and pipelines (bringing PMS/CRM data into our reporting stack)
- AI/ML model development (powering insights, summaries, and recommendations)

- Full end-to-end marketing automation (that's the AEP priority)
- Non-multifamily verticals (Student/Senior)
- Migration of all internal dashboards (focus on client-facing first)
